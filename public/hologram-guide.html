Key principle: Black = invisible on the pyramid. Only the colored, lit parts of the screen reflect and appear as the hologram. That's why your cocktail page must have a pure black background — the black disappears and only the cocktail floats.

Tablet Setup Instructions

  • 1

    Open Chrome on the tablet. Go to arwaikiki.vercel.app/hologram

  • 2

    Tap the three dots → Add to Home Screen. This creates a fullscreen shortcut with no browser chrome.

  • 3

    In Android Settings → Display → Screen Timeout → set to Never. Screen must stay on 24/7.

  • 4

    Install Fully Kiosk Browser (free on Play Store) to lock the tablet — guests cannot close the app or access other apps.

  • 5

    Place the tablet face-up inside the pyramid base. The pyramid sits on top. Adjust the tablet brightness to maximum for the best hologram visibility.

  • 6

    Keep the tablet plugged in permanently. Use a cable that routes cleanly through the table (tape it down so guests don't pull it).

Nightclub Environment Tips

💡 Lighting

Pepper's Ghost works best in dim lighting. Your nightclub environment is ideal. Avoid placing tables directly under strong spotlights — it washes out the hologram. Coloured ambient light (purple, blue) makes the hologram even more dramatic.

🔒 Security

Use Fully Kiosk Browser in kiosk mode so guests can't mess with the tablet. Consider a small lock or adhesive base so the pyramid can't be easily lifted. Keep a spare pyramid at the bar in case one gets broken.

📶 WiFi

The /hologram page fetches data every 30 seconds. A single WiFi router covers 20 tables easily. Separate your hologram tablet WiFi from guest WiFi for reliability.

🔋 Power

Keep tablets plugged in at all times — a screen-on, always-fetching browser drains battery fast. Run power cables under the table and tape neatly. Use a multi-socket extension under each table cluster.
