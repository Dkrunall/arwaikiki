🍹 Hiro AR Marker

Print this marker and place it on your table as a coaster. Point the Waikiki AR camera at it to see your cocktail card pop up in 3D.

Prints at 12 cm × 12 cm — ideal for detection

Printing Tips

  • Use matte paper — glossy causes glare and ruins tracking
  • Keep the full white border — don’t crop edges
  • Lay it flat on the table — curled paper breaks tracking
  • Hold phone 20–40 cm above the marker
  • Needs decent lighting — avoid dark environments
  • Laminating it makes it last much longer
